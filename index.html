Why Choose Us?

When you work for an NGO, you work for an organisation that requires skills like accounting, finance, research, raising and re-distribution of funds, punctuality, teamwork, cooperation and leadership qualities. You will gain experience whilst working for an NGO. You get a chance to build your network with influential people and bigger companies. It gives you a chance to know people with same goals as you. It can also increase your job prospects within the NGO or with the institutions that NGO work with.

Donations

Its a way of helping NGOs rise up and thus, the overall socio-economic status of the society you are a part of improves.Apart from providing satisfaction and happiness on an emotional level, donations made to eligible NGOs give you some other great rewards as well

Community

Being a part of a community can make the NGOs feel as that they are a part of something greater than themselves. It gives them opportunities to connect with people, to reach for thier goals, and makes the society feel safe and secure.

Outreach
